#Watey

Watey is a web application that will help me track how much water I am drinking a day and remember to drink water.

## tech stack

Frontend: React / TypeScript
Backend: Python
Database: postgres
Deployment: vercel + railway

## Functionality

users will be able to...

- create a account
- set how much water they want to drink a day
- see how much water they have drank so far (visually shown with some sort of water bottle display that fills up when pressed )
- track their water consumption on past days.

## Lifestyle

Once a user creates a account, a modal should appear that asks "how much water do you want to drink today". This modal should also state the universal amount of water bottles a person should drink a day for a reference. After selecting how much water they would like to drink, the modal should disappear and a empty bottle of water should appear. Every time a users drinks a full bottle of water, they will come back to the app and click on the bottle of water ti fill it up. the tracker for the day will end once the bottle is filled to the top. User could then head over the the calender to track all the other days.

## style

I would like the application to be minimalistic. since the app is about water and water is blue, I would like the site to have different shades of blue.
The application should also be easy to navigate (only having 3 tabs: calender, bottle and profile).

the profile page should just show the day the user created, the number of total bottles of water drank and a profile picture
